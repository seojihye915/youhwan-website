It is a media facade called "Resistance of Light" designed to celebrate the 77th anniversary of Korea's liberation from Japanese colonial rule. Media art expresses the pain and noble beliefs of our ancestors. The "Resistance of Light" performance is divided into four parts: peace, ordeal, belief, and liberation. The place where the media facade performance was held is Seodaemun Prison. This is where our ancestors were forcibly brought in during the struggle for independence during Japanese colonial era and actually imprisoned.
