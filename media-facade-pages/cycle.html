"Cycle"encapsulates the essence of emergence alongside the velocity of divergence, as well as the significance of harmony merging within the spectacle of discord. It prompts questions about our existence and meaning by exploring the similarity and connection between the movements of life we experience and those of the universe. By delving into the orbit between emotion and reason, beauty and contradiction, it guides us to gaze into the deeper depths of our inner selves.
