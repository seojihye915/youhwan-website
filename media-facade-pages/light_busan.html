A Step Of Hope

Even in the rain, if you keep moving forward, the world you wished for will unfold.

Eyes, Water, People

A drop of water falls before my eyes. In proportion to the passing time, unnecessary fragments pile up, frame by frame. This fleeting, lucid moment pulls me back again. What meaning do those drifting lights carry for me? The lights of Paris three years ago, and the damp, trailing figures of Amsterdam’s streets, rewind themselves and enter my space. There are too many useless things. And yet, I begin to wonder—am I the one who makes them useless?
